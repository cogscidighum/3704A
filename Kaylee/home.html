Digital Cognitive Celebrity Scientist: Anil Seth

Anil Seth is a famous digitial cognitive scientist, as well as a neuroscientist, and a professor of Cognitive and Computational Neuroscience at the University of Sussex in England. His research focuses on the nature of consciousness, perception, and self-awareness. He is well-known for his nontraditional theory of conscious experiences being controlled hallucinations generated by the brain in its role as a 'prediction engine'. Through his various books, podcasts, TED Talks, and media appearances, Anil Seth has become an influential figure in bridging the knowledge gap between science and the public by explaining the complexities of consciousness research to a larger audience.
